ABOUT

HELPING EDUCATORS TO NAVIGATE THEIR PROFRSSIONAL GROWTH WITH HIGH QUALITY CONTENT.

EDUPATH IS A STATIC AND USER-CENTRIC WEBSITE DESIGNED TO PROVIDE STUDENTS WITH A VISUAL AND STRUCTURE REPRESENTATION OF THEIR ACADEMIC/CAREER JOURNEY.

THIS INNOVATIVE PLATFORM TRANSFORMS THE TRADITIONAL COURSE CATALOG INTO AN INTERACTIVE ROUTE MAP,ENABLING STUDENTS TO NAVIGATE THROUGH THEIR CHOSEN ACADEMIC PATH.

THE MAIN AIM OF THIS PLATFORM IS TO PROVIDE DETAILED STRUCTURAL REPRESENTATION OF THE COMPLETE COURSE WITH THEIR REFERENCES.
